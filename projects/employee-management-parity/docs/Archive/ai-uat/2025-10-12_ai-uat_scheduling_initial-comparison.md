### Functional issues identified

1. **Missing toggles:** The real Naumen product has explicit day/period and coverage/adherence switches; these are absent in the demo. Only dataset tabs are present, and the small sigma/123 toggles have no effect on the charts.
   
2. **KPI gaps:** The demo displays only a coverage metric. Adherence and service‑level KPIs (and their trend arrows) are not implemented. Coverage updates when toggling between per‑employee and aggregated views but does not respond to other interactions.
   
3. **Inactive controls:** The green **“Построить”** button, blue **“FTE”** button and the sigma/123 toggles do not trigger any actions or visual changes. These controls should recalculate forecasts, toggle FTE/headcount overlays and recalc FTE like the real product.
   
4. **Non‑functional search filter:** Entering a skill name in the search box and applying the filter does not change the grid or charts, even when the skill does not exist![](https://sdmntprsouthcentralus.oaiusercontent.com/files/00000000-6178-61f7-bdbb-c199feeefd1a/raw?se=2025-10-12T13%3A40%3A13Z&sp=r&sv=2024-08-04&sr=b&scid=662487a9-14ac-5814-aca7-41f411f852c6&skoid=04233560-0ad7-493e-8bf0-1347c317d021&sktid=a48cca56-e6da-484e-a814-9c849652bcb3&skt=2025-10-12T13%3A26%3A46Z&ske=2025-10-13T13%3A26%3A46Z&sks=b&skv=2024-08-04&sig=MEarsalJSzmRMTcaljc7TW2AeUGAQQgLg5CeJx1uDGs%3D)schedule-grid-system-mock-oc2jc37u9-granins-projects.vercel.app.
   
5. **No tooltips:** Hovering over lines or bars on the forecast, deviation or service‑level charts does not reveal any tooltips with exact values, making it impossible to read precise metrics![](https://sdmntprsouthcentralus.oaiusercontent.com/files/00000000-5a4c-61f7-9455-d1f34210ea3d/raw?se=2025-10-12T13%3A34%3A50Z&sp=r&sv=2024-08-04&sr=b&scid=a2a0414e-c4b6-5745-beef-85347456ef10&skoid=04233560-0ad7-493e-8bf0-1347c317d021&sktid=a48cca56-e6da-484e-a814-9c849652bcb3&skt=2025-10-11T23%3A27%3A12Z&ske=2025-10-12T23%3A27%3A12Z&sks=b&skv=2024-08-04&sig=KyvAm6n3WEufz3n04vCuqzZ7O1aboJD6Wmn60b4UfXA%3D)schedule-grid-system-mock-oc2jc37u9-granins-projects.vercel.app.
   
6. **Lack of empty/error handling:** When filters yield no data or an invalid value is entered, the UI should show a graceful “нет данных” message. Currently the grid and charts stay unchanged, giving no feedback to the user.
   
7. **Limited accessibility:** While controls are keyboard reachable, there are no focus indicators on toggles and no accessible text describing the charts. Screen‑reader users would struggle to interpret the data.
