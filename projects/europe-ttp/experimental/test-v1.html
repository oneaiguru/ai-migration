When Gurudev mentioned that if you want to learn anything deeply, you have to teach it. I am a teacher by profession. To strengthen my own sadhana and in the process if I can spread bliss around. More than anything else, to get out of the comfort zone.
 
In one of the satsangh video, Gurudev mentioned become a teacher, you will be polished more and the best way of sadhana is to teach. Till that moment I considered myself to be ineligible to teach. But, after that moment, I was ready and open
 
Gurudev mentions about ante vaasi - devotee living in the heart of Gurudev. I had made a prayer for myself and Gurudev to cure non-alcoholic fatty liver syndrome (I assumed he has it because he stomach was getting big like mine due to cholesterol medication) to Lord Vaidhyanathaswamy. As I was traveling to Vaidheeswaran Kovil, my aunt mentioned this place is Papanasam, and I heard it in my sleep. I woke up and in next two minutes, we visited Gurudev's birth place. After the we reached the temple and offered our prayers. I did not know Papanasam will be on the way. Gurudev just revealed that HE is holding me in HIS heart.
 
How life was full of misery and without direction without a Guru? Now, with Gurudev, I have direction, purpose, HIS blessings, fearlessness
 
Till July 2020, I was employed as an Associate Professor of Finance in the University of Akron. Though it was supposed to be a tenured position considered very secure, and despite my good performance, i was laid off. So, right now, my full time job is to see whether I am living by all the knowledge, living in the present moment, taking full responsibility, without blaming anyone, and opposite values are complementary, and smiling through all, come what may, and knowing full well that a higher power protects me, that everything is changing during this period of grand transformation of the world, this experience is making me stronger, and it is for a new direction, which I would not have done / taken voluntarily because I had a secure job.

I chose not to marry or have children. But, all students are my children. My family, all of them, including my nieces and nephews, are very supportive of my Art of Living journey and they are also traveling with me.
 
Positive attitude, enthusiasm for life, organized, full of love, attitude of learn from experience and move on

Gurudev, who else. I thought I was very set in my ways, till I met Gurudev. The transformation Gurudev brought in me is full of love, easy, step by step, timely, and many things that I did not even know.
 
I either live by myself or with my mother, when she visits me. So, all the work at home and in the workplace will have to be done by me. When I am working in a job, I was teaching and doing research. I still do research. Art of Living activities are my responsibilities and I have enough time to complete all.
 
Please accept people and situations as they are, including yourself. Though I do this more easily now, for me personally, it was a long journey to get to this point. I was always expecting others to do favors / help for me and will get disappointed and angry when those expectations were not met, which led to constant resentments, leaving good friends, and to my own unhappiness and getting angry with myself. After I did the first Sudarshan Kriya, my fears went away and I started trusting myself and others. I started accepting people who held views that are diametrically opposite of mine, because I knew now that they are complementary and God loves variety and the juice of life comes from these differences and I develop my own skills.
 
Fear of death went away. I am comfortable in my own skin. I do not get irritated or angry so easily these days. For example, I know full well that there are no reasons for my layoff last month, but I completely trust the Divine for this experience and growth. Without all the knowledge I received from Gurudev, in small dozes, very frequently and at the most appropriate time, I will not be where I am today. I am here to accept that and not ask Gurudev to find me a good job or get me back in the old job etc.
 
That said, I am looking for jobs and I do not know where or when I will land in one. So, I will certainly be able to do the weekend TTC courses. I request the TTC Desk to work with me if I have to be in a new job when the face to face course starts. I am again leaving it to the Divine will.
