In 2006 I was working with a small company. We meet Guruji at the ashram and he asked everyone to come for silver jubilee celebration in India. My wife and kids have booked their tickets but I was not sure to go as I have a decent job after years of struggle in Canada, and do not want to take vacation. The company says no to my vacation, so I resigned that moment with giving them two week notice and book my flight. After resigning from job in few hours I got a call from my old company that I used to work but for very less money. I asked them more money and joining date after Silver Jubilee. Same month I have closing, as the new job might affect my mortgage approval, so as per my consultants advice I asked them to give a letter saying I am permanent employee for the mortgage purpose. What a grace! There are many other incidents too, our whole life is become celebration now.
